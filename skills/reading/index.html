Reading Strategies for SDS

Core Principle: Scan, Don’t Read The SDS reading section rewards finding specific information quickly, not understanding every word. Train your eyes to scan for keywords. Strategy 1: Read Questions First Before reading any text: Read ALL questions or statements Underline/note the keywords in each question Then scan the text for those keywords This turns reading into a search task rather than a comprehension task.
