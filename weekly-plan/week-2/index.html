Week 2 — A1/A2 Writing + Listening Basics

Inhaltsverzeichnis
  • Day 1 (Mon) — Vocabulary: Shopping + Writing
  • Day 2 (Tue) — Vocabulary: Health + Listening
  • Day 3 (Wed) — Writing: Simple Emails
  • Day 4 (Thu) — Vocabulary: Children + Speaking
  • Day 5 (Fri) — Writing: More Emails + Listening
  • Day 6 (Sat) — Mixed Practice
  • Day 7 (Sun) — Rest or Light Review
  • Week 2 Summary Checklist

Goal: Build writing confidence with messages and simple emails. Train basic listening comprehension.

Day 1 (Mon) — Vocabulary: Shopping + Writing#

Time Activity Resource
15 min Study Vocab Pack 04 — Shopping 02-vocabulary/04-shopping.md
15 min Write 2 text message replies (Prompts 1–2) 03-skills/writing/practice-prompts.md
15 min Number dictation drill 03-skills/listening/practice-drills.md
  • Done

Day 2 (Tue) — Vocabulary: Health + Listening#

Time Activity Resource
15 min Study Vocab Pack 05 — Health 02-vocabulary/05-health.md
20 min Short dialogue comprehension (doctor/shop scenarios) 03-skills/listening/practice-drills.md
15 min Review vocab packs 4–5 Self-test
  • Done

Day 3 (Wed) — Writing: Simple Emails#

Time Activity Resource
15 min Review email templates (A2 section) 03-skills/writing/email-templates.md
20 min Write 2 simple emails (Prompts 9–10) 03-skills/writing/practice-prompts.md
15 min Listening: announcement comprehension 03-skills/listening/practice-drills.md
  • Done

Day 4 (Thu) — Vocabulary: Children + Speaking#

Time Activity Resource
15 min Study Vocab Pack 06 — Children 02-vocabulary/06-children.md
15 min Practice self-introduction (record 2×) 03-skills/speaking/self-introduction.md
15 min Practice “typical day” draft 03-skills/speaking/typical-day.md
  • Done

Day 5 (Fri) — Writing: More Emails + Listening#

Time Activity Resource
20 min Write 2 emails (Prompts 11–12) 03-skills/writing/practice-prompts.md
15 min One-pass listening summary drill 03-skills/listening/practice-drills.md
15 min Review vocab packs 4–6 Self-test
  • Done

Day 6 (Sat) — Mixed Practice#

Time Activity Resource
15 min Write 2 text message replies (Prompts 3–5) 03-skills/writing/practice-prompts.md
15 min Reading Exercise 5 (Form fill) 03-skills/reading/practice-exercises.md
20 min Record “typical day” (aim for 2–3 minutes) 03-skills/speaking/typical-day.md
  • Done

Day 7 (Sun) — Rest or Light Review#

Time Activity
15 min Re-read your written emails — fix mistakes
15 min Listen back to your speaking recordings
  • Done

Week 2 Summary Checklist#

  • Completed Vocab Pack 04 — Shopping
  • Completed Vocab Pack 05 — Health
  • Completed Vocab Pack 06 — Children
  • Wrote 5 text message replies (A1)
  • Wrote 4+ simple emails (A2)
  • Did at least 4 listening drills
  • Practiced self-introduction (fluent without notes)
  • Drafted and recorded “typical day”
