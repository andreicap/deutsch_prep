Goal: Simulate real test conditions 2–3 times. Identify and fix your weak areas.

Day 1 (Mon) — Full Mock Test 1#

Time Activity Notes
30 min Reading: Do all 5 practice exercises (timed) 03-skills/reading/practice-exercises.md
30 min Listening: 3 drills back-to-back (no pausing) 03-skills/listening/practice-drills.md
20 min Writing: Write 1 email + 1 opinion text (timed) Random from 03-skills/writing/practice-prompts.md
15 min Speaking: Record self-intro + 1 process + 1 pro/contra Random from 03-skills/speaking/

After the mock: Fill in the error log below.
