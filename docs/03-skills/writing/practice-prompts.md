# Writing Practice Prompts

> 30 writing prompts organized by level. Write each one, then compare with the templates in `email-templates.md`.

---

## A1 — Text Message Replies (5 prompts)

### Prompt 1
> Nachricht: "Hallo! Hast du am Samstag Zeit? Wir machen ein Grillfest."
>
> Write a reply (2–3 sentences).

### Prompt 2
> Nachricht: "Hi! Wo treffen wir uns morgen?"
>
> Write a reply — suggest a meeting place and time.

### Prompt 3
> Nachricht: "Hey, kannst du mir bitte Milch mitbringen? Danke!"
>
> Write a reply — confirm or say you can't.

### Prompt 4
> Nachricht: "Kommst du heute zum Deutschkurs? Um 18 Uhr."
>
> Write a reply — you are sick and can't come.

### Prompt 5
> Nachricht: "Hallo! Ich habe eine neue Telefonnummer: 079 345 67 89. Speicher sie bitte!"
>
> Write a reply — confirm and ask how the person is doing.

## A2 — Simple Emails (10 prompts)

### Prompt 6: Cancel a doctor's appointment
> You have an appointment on Thursday at 10:00. You can't come because you have to work. Write an email: apologize, give a reason, ask for a new appointment.

### Prompt 7: Ask about a course
> You want to take a swimming course. Write to the Schwimmbad: ask about days, times, and price.

### Prompt 8: Respond to an invitation
> Your neighbor invites you to a street party on Saturday. Write: accept the invitation, ask what you should bring, ask about the time.

### Prompt 9: Cancel a reservation
> You reserved a table at a restaurant for Friday evening. You can't come. Write to the restaurant: cancel, apologize, ask if you can come next week instead.

### Prompt 10: Report a problem
> Your internet doesn't work. Write to the provider: describe the problem, say since when, ask for help.

### Prompt 11: Ask about lost item
> You left your bag on the bus. Write to the transport company: describe the bag, say which bus and when, ask if they found it.

### Prompt 12: Reply to a job ad
> You see a job ad for a cleaner (50%, Monday–Wednesday). Write: say you're interested, mention your experience, ask when you can start.

### Prompt 13: Thank someone
> Your German teacher helped you a lot. Write a thank-you email: say what you learned, thank them, wish them well.

### Prompt 14: Ask about opening hours
> You want to visit the Ausländeramt. Write: ask about opening hours, whether you need an appointment, and what documents to bring.

### Prompt 15: Complain about a product
> You ordered shoes online and they are the wrong size. Write to the shop: explain the problem, ask for an exchange or refund.

## B1 — Request / Inquiry Emails (10 prompts)

### Prompt 16: Ask for vacation
> Write to your employer. Ask for 5 days vacation from 15–19 July. Give a reason (family visit). Offer to finish your work before you leave.

### Prompt 17: Explain absence from school event
> Write to your child's teacher. You can't attend the parents' evening on Tuesday. Explain why. Ask for the information by email. Suggest another time for a meeting.

### Prompt 18: Ask about a German course
> Write to a language school. Ask about: levels available, prices, schedule, start dates, class size.

### Prompt 19: Request a reference letter
> Write to your former employer. Ask for a reference letter (Arbeitszeugnis). Explain that you need it for a new job application. Ask how long it will take.

### Prompt 20: Reschedule a job interview
> Write to HR. Your interview is on Monday at 9:00. You can't come — your child is sick. Apologize. Suggest two alternative dates.

### Prompt 21: Report a noise problem
> Write to your landlord. Your neighbors are very loud every evening after 22:00. Describe the noise. Ask the landlord to speak to them.

### Prompt 22: Ask about an apartment
> You found an apartment ad online. Write to the landlord: ask about the exact rent, Nebenkosten, availability date, and whether pets are allowed. Ask for a viewing appointment.

### Prompt 23: Request training/Weiterbildung
> Write to your employer. You want to take a 3-day Excel course. Explain why it would help your work. Ask if the company will pay for it.

### Prompt 24: Cancel a membership
> Write to your gym (Fitnessstudio). You want to cancel your membership because you're moving. Ask about the Kündigungsfrist. Ask if you'll get a refund for the remaining months.

### Prompt 25: Ask for help from Beratungsstelle
> Write to a counseling center (Beratungsstelle). You need help finding a job. Briefly describe your situation (new in Switzerland, trained as X). Ask for an appointment.

## B2 — Opinion / Pro-Contra Texts (5 prompts)

### Prompt 26: Sunday shopping
> *Sollten Geschäfte am Sonntag geöffnet sein?*
> Write ~200 words: arguments for and against, your opinion with reasons.

### Prompt 27: Home office
> *Sollten Arbeitnehmer das Recht auf Home-Office haben?*
> Write ~200 words: advantages and disadvantages, your conclusion.

### Prompt 28: Smartphones for children
> *Ab welchem Alter sollten Kinder ein Smartphone haben?*
> Write ~200 words: pros and cons, your recommendation.

### Prompt 29: Public transport vs. cars
> *Sollte man den öffentlichen Verkehr gratis machen, um die Umwelt zu schützen?*
> Write ~200 words: arguments for and against, your opinion.

### Prompt 30: Workplace training
> *Sollten Arbeitgeber Weiterbildungen für alle Mitarbeitenden bezahlen?*
> Write ~200 words: advantages and disadvantages, your position.
